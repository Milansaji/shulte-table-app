# Schulte Master

Improve your concentration, speed, and visual perception with Schulte Master!

Challenge your brain by finding numbers in sequence as fast as possible. Track your progress, beat your best scores, and compete with others.

## Features:

* Multiple grid sizes (3x3 to 10x10)
* Daily challenges
* Leaderboards
* Light & Dark mode
* Performance tracking
* Smooth and minimal UI

Perfect for students, professionals, and anyone looking to boost mental performance.

Train daily. Think faster. Stay sharp.
